FCFS

First-Come First-Served

Characteristics

  • Simple FIFO queue
  • Non-preemptive
  • O(1) scheduling

Limitation

Convoy effect; no service differentiation

Priority

Priority-based + Aging

Characteristics

  • 4-level priority (URGENT~LOW)
  • Aging for starvation prevention
  • Dynamic priority adjustment

Limitation

Potential starvation without aging

MLFQ

Multi-Level Feedback Queue

Characteristics

  • Adaptive classification
  • Time quantum per level
  • Preemptive scheduling

Limitation

Parameter tuning complexity

WFQ

Weighted Fair Queuing

Characteristics

  • Weight-proportional service
  • Virtual Finish Time
  • JFI fairness metric

Limitation

Computational overhead of VFT

Simple

Implementation Complexity / Performance Optimization →
